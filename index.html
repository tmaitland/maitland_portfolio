A little about me

I have spent most of my life with my face in a book, in front of a computer screen, in a sketchpad--drawing, or in a notebook--writing. Despite this, I have managed to find myself outdoors to enjoy nature and interact with people. I consider myself to be an intellectual and I enjoy fields of study where the learning possibilities are endless--hence my pursuit of web development. I am also an empath, and I often strive to ensure that everyone is realistically content with their current circumstances. I am also willing to help others resolve any conflicts or fix inefficiencies.

I've got skills

Full Stack Developer

As a teen, mySpace, blogs, and forums were the extent of my exposure to coding. Since then, I have built my own website from scatch using HTML and CSS. Now, I'm in a coding school called TechLaunch, where I have learned to build small web applications with Node and React.

Designer

I've always doodled on my classwork as a kid, and I've spent much of my teenage years drawing. Now, in the digital age, I use my eye for detail to design interfaces for the web applications and webpages that I create. I use Illustrator to create layouts or logos, but mostly, I use my trusty sketchbook.

Writer

From poems, stories, journals, and of course papers, I have been writing my whole life. It's exciting to elicit emotions, thoughts, and supply knowledge with words alone, which is what I do as a writer. As an author of code, or a programmer, I can also do this with websites and the programs I build and run.
